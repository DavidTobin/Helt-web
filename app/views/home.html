How Does This Help?

Visual Data

We've found that the biggest cause associated with weight gain/loss is a lack of calorie control. Weight loss is achieved by taking in less calories (energy) than you burn. When presented with data, showing your efforts, we believe people will make conscious efforts to maintain proper control.

The beauty of Helt is that it can calculate approximately how many calories you need per day, and help you track it. It can give you estimations of weight loss based on a few pieces of data you provide for us.

Scientific Calculation

Helt uses an equation called the Mifflin Equation for Weight Control. This is the most accurate method for calculating resting energy expenditure in healthy individuals. We combine this equation with energy expenditure from exercise to predict your weight loss.
